FAQ so you have some questions...

Why does everyone have wings?
I'm probably running Genesis' dress up app.
How do I add music to the main screen?
There is a triangluar play button near your menu that allows you to search for music on youtube. Songs will then play in order that they were sent to the queue.
Will I have my outfit if I leave the room?
Short answer: No. If someone else is running the dress up app in their room you will automatically have the same outfit you put on, but by default most rooms are not using it.
